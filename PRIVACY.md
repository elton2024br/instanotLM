# Privacy Policy

## Overview

InstaNotLM is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data when you use our extension.

## What Data We Collect

### Data We Extract (From Instagram)
- **Public Instagram profile information**: username, name, bio, followers count, posts, captions, hashtags, mentions
- **Post data**: creation date, engagement metrics (likes, comments), location tags
- **Image descriptions**: automated captions generated by Instagram

**Important:** We only extract data from PUBLIC profiles you choose to analyze.

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- Your Instagram password
- Your email address
- Private messages or DMs
- Follower/Following lists (private)
- Stories or Highlights (private)
- Comments on private accounts
- Payment or financial information

## How We Use Your Data

1. **Local Processing**: All extracted data is processed locally on your computer
2. **Document Generation**: We format the data into Markdown documents
3. **Export**: You download the file to your computer
4. **Analysis**: You can upload the file to Google NotebookLM for AI analysis

**We do not:**
- Send your data to external servers
- Store your data on cloud services
- Share your data with third parties
- Use your data for analytics or tracking
- Use your data for any purpose other than what you explicitly request

## Browser Session

- The extension uses your existing Instagram browser session
- We leverage your authentication to access public data
- Your Instagram credentials remain with Instagram, not stored in InstaNotLM

## Exported Files

- Files are saved to your local **Downloads** folder
- You have complete control over where files are stored
- We recommend deleting sensitive files after analysis
- Consider using encrypted storage for sensitive investigations

## Third-Party Services

InstaNotLM does NOT integrate with:
- Analytics services
- Cloud storage services (unless you manually upload)
- Ad networks
- Data brokers
- External APIs (except Instagram's public APIs)

## Google NotebookLM Integration

When you upload files to Google NotebookLM:
- That's a Google service, not controlled by InstaNotLM
- Review Google's privacy policy for their data handling
- You control what files you upload
- Google handles your data according to their terms

## Data Security

### How We Protect Data
- Chrome Manifest V3 sandboxing
- No network transmission of extracted data
- Local processing only
- No databases or servers

### Your Responsibility
- Keep your Chrome browser updated
- Secure your computer from unauthorized access
- Use strong Instagram account security
- Delete sensitive exported files

## Your Rights

- **Access**: You have complete access to all data extracted
- **Control**: You decide what data to extract and how to use it
- **Deletion**: You can delete extracted data anytime
- **Transparency**: This policy is transparent about our practices
